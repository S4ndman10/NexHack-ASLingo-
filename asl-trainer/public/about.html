About Us

Our journey started with a simple idea: to make learning American Sign Language (ASL) accessible to everyone. We were inspired by the film A Sound of Metal to come up with a way to bridge the communication gap that leaves individuals with heading-imparities at a disadvantage in society.

By leveraging MediaPipe Hands and real-time webcam feedback, our AI trained ASL Trainer allows learners to practice gestures and receive instant visual feedback, making the learning experience interactive and fun while they achieve a meaningful goal of being able to communicate without using their voice.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge communication gaps, empower individuals, and foster understanding between the hearing and Deaf communities. Learning ASL should be easy, engaging, and available to anyone with a computer or webcam.

Key Features

Real-Time Hand Tracking

Practice ASL gestures with live feedback as the system tracks each finger and wrist accurately using your webcam.

Dark & Light Mode Support

The interface adapts to your preferred system theme for a comfortable learning experience day or night.

Interactive Learning

Engage with exercises and see your progress instantly, making it easier to master the ASL alphabet and gestures.
